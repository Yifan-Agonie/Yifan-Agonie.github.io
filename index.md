<h1 id="disaster">Disaster Relief App</h1>

<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>
Designed a simplified disaster relief registration experience to help users access support quickly under high-stress conditions.
</p>

<h2>Problem</h2>
<p>
Users faced a complex, text-heavy registration process that increased cognitive load and slowed down access to critical support.
</p>

<img src="images/journey.png">
<p><em>Figure 1. User journey highlighting pain points under stress.</em></p>

<h2>Research</h2>
<p>
Conducted user interviews and usability testing to identify key behavioural challenges and decision-making barriers.
</p>

<img src="images/research.png">
<p><em>Figure 2. Research synthesis identifying key usability issues.</em></p>

<h2>Key Insights</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Users struggle with complex forms under stress</li>
  <li>Too much information increases drop-off</li>
  <li>Clear guidance is critical</li>
</ul>

<h2>Design Solutions</h2>

<h3>Simplified Step-by-Step Flow</h3>

<p>
The registration process was redesigned into a guided step-by-step flow to reduce cognitive load and improve usability.
</p>

<div class="row">
  <img src="images/step1.png">
  <img src="images/step2.png">
